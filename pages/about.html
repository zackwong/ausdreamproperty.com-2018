AUSDREAM is a property group with broad access to capital invested in quality assets and development projects which, through proactive management, creates great customer experiences and superior returns.

AUSDREAM PROPERTY is a development company engaged in land acquisition, design, construction, and sales of medium to high density luxury residential townhouses & apartments & mixed use spaces in the Brisbane & Gold Coast area of Queensland Australia.

AUSDREAM PROPERTY strategically focused on providing "affordable luxury living" residence. The residence is considered to have modern design and positive long term structural characteristics, with an enduring customer need and effective capital utilisation.
